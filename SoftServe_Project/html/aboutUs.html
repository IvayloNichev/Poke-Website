About Us

Poke is a traditional Hawaiian dish that consists of diced raw fish, usually tuna or salmon, served with a variety of seasonings and sauces. It is often accompanied by rice, vegetables, and other toppings. Poke is known for its fresh, healthy ingredients and bold flavors.

Poke originated in Hawaii, where it has been a staple for centuries. The word "poke" means "to slice" or "cut" in Hawaiian, referring to the way the fish is prepared. Traditionally, poke was made with fish that was caught fresh from the ocean and seasoned with salt, seaweed, and roasted kukui nut. Over time, it has evolved to include various influences and ingredients from different cultures.

Poke-Chew is a contemporary restaurant dedicated to bringing the authentic flavors of Hawaiian poke to your table. Our team is passionate about using the freshest ingredients to create delicious and healthy poke bowls that cater to a variety of tastes and dietary preferences. We believe in the power of good food to bring people together and create memorable experiences. Whether you're a poke enthusiast or trying it for the first time, we welcome you to join us on this culinary journey.
